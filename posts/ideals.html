Ideals

Are you truly free?

  • Will you stay with nothing but your ideals?
  • Will you bring any value, or only your ideals?
  • Will your ideals struggle with you?

Are you truly able?

  • Will you follow your ideals even with the person you love?
  • Will you sell your dusty old laptop?
  • Will you leave that job — the one that gives you your "freedom" — to go live by yourself with nothing but the value you accumulated to make you "free"?

Are you truly strong?

  • Will your ideals change if they are proven at fault?
  • Will you have the courage to disagree?
  • Will you stick to your ideals when it matters most?
