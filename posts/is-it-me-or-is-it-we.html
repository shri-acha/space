Is it me or is it we?

I'm always amazed by how our society has developed and how we see value.

I've always seen value as something that benefits me.

But if one thinks about it, it is never about me, rather it is about us and how we as a society have developed over the years.

Only now that I have come to realize that I'm not being valued for what I do for myself rather what I have done for the society or for the species.

I'm not being valued in a group for the work I have done for myself, but for what change I contributed to.

"Love as a principle, order as a foundation, progress as a goal" ~Auguste Comte
